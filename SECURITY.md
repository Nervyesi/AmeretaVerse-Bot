# Security

AVbot processes server data including Discord member information, X (Twitter) handles submitted by users, and wallet addresses submitted by users. We care about handling this data responsibly.

## Reporting a security concern

If you discover a security issue that affects AVbot users, please report it privately rather than disclosing it publicly.

Contact:
- Email: ameretaverse@gmail.com with the subject "Security Disclosure"
- Discord DM: nervyesi1

Please include:
- A clear description of the concern
- Steps to confirm or reproduce it
- Any potential impact you have considered

We will acknowledge receipt within a reasonable time and respond with our assessment.

## What we cover

Reports about the live AVbot service at api.avbot.app, the dashboard at www.avbot.app/dashboard, and the public bot operating under the AVbot name are within scope.

Reports about forks, self hosted copies, third party services, or unrelated tools are not within our scope.

## What we ask

- Do not exploit any finding against real users, real servers, or real data
- Do not access, modify, or destroy data that does not belong to you
- Do not run automated scans that produce load on the production service
- Keep the details of a finding private until we have had a reasonable chance to respond

## Recognition

Researchers who report a real, verifiable issue in good faith and follow this policy will be credited (with their permission) and may receive a recognition role in the AmeretaVerse community Discord. We do not currently offer monetary rewards.

## Public disclosure

Once a reported issue is resolved, we may publish a brief acknowledgement of the report. We will coordinate the timing and detail with the reporter.
